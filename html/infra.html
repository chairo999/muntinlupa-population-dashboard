Power — Meralco Electrification

Muntinlupa City • Source: Meralco – Utility Economics & CPDO, 2023

The City of Muntinlupa's sole electric provider is Meralco (Manila Electric Railroad and Light Company). Meralco is the largest private electric distribution utility company in the Philippines, serving 39 cities and 72 municipalities with over 7,000 sq. km. of franchise area. Although it covers only about 3% of the country's land area, it supplies approximately 55% of the Philippines' electricity output. As of 2024, Meralco has been operating for 121 years.
